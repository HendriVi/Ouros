Overview

We broker the resale of refurbished, up-to-date medical equipment. Our job is simple: connect buyers with reliable devices, provide clear documentation, and coordinate a clean handover—so you can equip your facility without the delays and cost of buying new.

What you can expect

  • Verified functionality (inspection and testing via qualified service partners)
  • Transparent condition grading, photos, and included accessories
  • Documentation where available (service reports, manuals, compliance/marking records)
  • Coordinated logistics and handover paperwork

If you already know what you need, send us the make/model, quantity, destination, and timeline. We respond with options, availability, and pricing.

Process

1) Define the requirement. Tell us what you need (device type, preferred make/model, quantity), your timeline, and where it needs to be delivered.

2) We source and verify. We locate suitable units across our network and send a short dossier covering condition, included accessories, test status, and documentation.

3) You approve; we coordinate delivery. Once you select an option, we manage the transaction flow, pickup, packing, and shipping—then remain available for post‑delivery questions.

Common categories we place
Patient monitoring, ECG, ultrasound, infusion and syringe pumps, lab equipment, sterilization peripherals, and clinic infrastructure. Availability varies—ask.

Why refurbished

Refurbished equipment can be the fastest, most capital‑efficient way to expand capacity, replace aging devices, or standardize a fleet—without compromising on basic safety and functionality expectations. The key is verification and documentation.

Our focus is risk reduction:

  • Only current, serviceable models—no “museum pieces”
  • Inspection, cleaning, and functional testing via qualified partners
  • Serial numbers and device status recorded for traceability
  • Documentation packaged for your internal QA/biomed review

Tell us your compliance requirements up front (country, facility type, internal standards). We will only present options that match what you can actually deploy.
